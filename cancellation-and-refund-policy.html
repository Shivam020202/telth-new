CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: 20th May 2025

Last Updated: 20th May 2025

1. OVERVIEW

This Cancellation and Refund Policy ("Policy") governs the cancellation of services and refund procedures for Telth Healthcare Private Limited ("Telth", "we", "us", or "our") operating in India. This policy applies to all services including but not limited to consultations, diagnostic tests, care plans, home visits, telemedicine services, and product purchases through our platform.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES COVERED

This policy applies to:

  • Medical consultations (in-person and telemedicine)
  • Diagnostic tests and health examinations
  • Care plans and subscription services
  • Home care visits by Care Managers (CM) and Community Care Managers (CCM)
  • E-pharmacy orders and medication delivery
  • Training and certification programs
  • Medical device purchases and rentals
  • Emergency and urgent care services

3. CANCELLATION POLICY BY SERVICE TYPE

3.1 Medical Consultations

In-Person Consultations:

  • 24+ hours advance notice: Full refund minus 2% payment gateway charges
  • 12-24 hours advance notice: 75% refund
  • 6-12 hours advance notice: 50% refund
  • Less than 6 hours or no-show: No refund

Telemedicine Consultations:

  • 2+ hours advance notice: Full refund minus 2% payment gateway charges
  • 30 minutes to 2 hours advance notice: 50% refund
  • Less than 30 minutes or no-show: No refund

3.2 Diagnostic Tests and Health Examinations

Home-based Diagnostics:

  • 24+ hours advance notice: Full refund minus processing charges (₹50 or 2%, whichever is higher)
  • 12-24 hours advance notice: 80% refund
  • 6-12 hours advance notice: 50% refund
  • Less than 6 hours: 25% refund (to cover CM/CCM mobilization costs)
  • After sample collection: No refund

Center-based Diagnostics:

  • 12+ hours advance notice: Full refund minus ₹100 processing fee
  • 4-12 hours advance notice: 75% refund
  • Less than 4 hours or no-show: No refund

3.3 Care Plans and Subscriptions

Monthly Care Plans:

  • Cancellation allowed within 7 days of purchase with full refund
  • After 7 days: Pro-rated refund for unused portion minus 15% administrative charges
  • No refund after 50% of the plan period has elapsed

Annual Care Plans:

  • 15-day cooling-off period from purchase date with full refund
  • After 15 days: Pro-rated refund for unused portion minus 20% administrative charges
  • No refund after 6 months of the plan period

3.4 Home Care Visits (CM/CCM Services)

Scheduled Home Visits:

  • 4+ hours advance notice: Full refund minus travel reimbursement (if CM/CCM has started journey)
  • 2-4 hours advance notice: 75% refund
  • Less than 2 hours: 50% refund
  • After CM/CCM arrival: No refund

Emergency Home Visits:

  • Before CM/CCM dispatch: Full refund minus ₹200 coordination fee
  • After dispatch but before arrival: 50% refund plus actual travel costs
  • After arrival: No refund

3.5 E-Pharmacy Orders

Before Order Processing:

  • Prescription medicines: Full refund within 2 hours of order placement
  • OTC medicines: Full refund within 4 hours of order placement

After Order Processing:

  • Before dispatch: 90% refund (10% restocking fee)
  • After dispatch but before delivery: 75% refund plus return shipping charges
  • After delivery: Returns accepted only for damaged/expired products as per pharmaceutical regulations

3.6 Medical Devices and Equipment

Purchase:

  • Before shipment: Full refund minus 5% restocking fee
  • After delivery: 7-day return policy for unused items in original packaging (buyer bears return shipping)
  • Defective products: Full refund within 30 days

Rental:

  • Before delivery: Full refund minus booking charges
  • After delivery: Pro-rated refund for unused rental period minus pickup charges

4. EMERGENCY AND FORCE MAJEURE SITUATIONS

4.1 Medical Emergencies

In case of patient medical emergencies requiring immediate hospitalization:

  • Full refund for all pre-booked services
  • No cancellation charges apply
  • Medical certificate from treating physician required

4.2 Force Majeure Events

During events beyond reasonable control (natural disasters, government lockdowns, etc.):

  • Services may be rescheduled without penalty
  • Full refunds available if rescheduling not possible
  • Credit notes valid for 12 months may be offered as alternative

5. REFUND PROCESSING

5.1 Refund Timeline

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • UPI/Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days
  • Cash payments: Processed through bank transfer within 7-10 business days

5.2 Refund Method

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method
  • For cash payments, refunds will be processed via bank transfer (bank details required)
  • Processing fees and gateway charges are non-refundable unless specified otherwise

5.3 Payout Impact on Stakeholder Compensation

When refunds are processed:

  • CM/CCM payments will be adjusted in subsequent payouts
  • Doctor consultation fees will be reversed if consultation hasn't occurred
  • Diagnostic center payments will be withheld until service completion
  • Platform fees and commissions will be adjusted accordingly

6. CANCELLATION PROCESS

6.1 How to Cancel

Cancellations can be initiated through:

  • Telth mobile application
  • Customer care helpline: 18005700140
  • Website: www.mytelth.com
  • Direct communication with assigned CM/CCM (for home visits)

6.2 Required Information

  • Booking/Order ID
  • Registered mobile number
  • Reason for cancellation
  • Supporting documents (if applicable)

6.3 Confirmation

  • Cancellation confirmation will be sent via SMS and email
  • Refund reference number will be provided
  • Customer can track refund status through the app/website

7. NON-REFUNDABLE SERVICES

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Completed consultations and diagnostic tests
  • Used portions of care plans and subscriptions
  • Emergency services after provider mobilization
  • Training programs after completion of 25% curriculum
  • Digital health reports and second opinions
  • Platform convenience fees
  • Payment gateway charges (except where specified)

8. SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

8.1 Technical Issues

If service cancellation is due to technical issues on Telth's platform:

  • Full refund without any deductions
  • Expedited refund processing within 24-48 hours
  • Complimentary rescheduling if desired

8.2 Provider Unavailability

If cancellation is due to healthcare provider unavailability:

  • Full refund or free rescheduling
  • Compensation equivalent to 10% of service fee (up to ₹500)
  • Priority booking for rescheduled appointments

8.3 Prescription-Related Cancellations

If consultation is cancelled due to prescription issues:

  • Medicines: Full refund if not dispatched
  • Follow-up consultation: Free rescheduling within 7 days

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

9.1 Grievance Redressal

  • First level: Customer care team (Response within 24 hours)
  • Second level: Operations manager (Response within 48 hours)
  • Third level: Senior management (Response within 72 hours)

  • Users will be notified of significant changes via email/SMS

  • Changes are effective immediately upon publication
